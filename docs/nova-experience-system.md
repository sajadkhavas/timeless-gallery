# NOVA Experience System

## Brand idea

**Time is a signature.** NOVA is not framed as a normal watch shop; it is a quiet digital gallery where each watch is treated as a personal statement.

## Experience principles

1. **Gallery before commerce** — large imagery, silence, controlled bronze accents, and deliberate whitespace.
2. **Trust before pressure** — authenticity, warranty, shipping, service, and expert support appear before aggressive sales copy.
3. **Motion with purpose** — reveal, hover, and section transitions clarify hierarchy; nothing moves just for decoration.
4. **Conversion without noise** — every key page has one primary next step and one softer secondary step.

## Visual language

- Deep charcoal background, ivory text, controlled bronze CTA/accent.
- Thin hairlines instead of heavy cards.
- Editorial image crops and restrained gradients.
- High contrast and clear focus states for keyboard users.

## Page journey

- Home: positive shock, brand promise, signature piece, pillars, collections, featured products, editorial trust.
- Collections: category worlds with short narrative and direct entry to shop filters.
- Shop: clean filtering, sort, product status, wishlist/compare-ready product cards.
- Product detail: image-first emotional confirmation, price/CTA, technical data, story, assurance, reviews and related products.
- Compare: decision support through visual and technical differences.
- Journal: SEO and authority through magazine-like education.
- Cart/Checkout: minimum friction, visible summary and reassurance.

## Motion rules

- Reveal sections once; avoid loops except subtle hover shimmer.
- Hover reveals information, never hides critical data.
- Mobile gets lighter motion and direct content order.
- 3D/WebGL should be lazy-loaded in the future with a static editorial fallback.
